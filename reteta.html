Sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice (鮨飯, sushi-meshi), usually with some sugar and salt, plus a variety of ingredients (ねた, neta), such as vegetables, and any meat, but most commonly seafood (often raw but can be cooked)

Basic components:

  • Rice
  • Nori
  • Salmon
  • Vegetables
  • Rice vinegar
  • Wasabi

Steps in preparing sushi include

  1. Rinse and cook the rice
  2. Cool and dry the rice
  3. Start shaping the rice
  4. Put rice on the algae
